Inhabitants of remote quarters of Chicago have a legend about a mysterious maniac named Candyman, an inhabitant of a parallel world. He does not need a knife to inflict wounds: a hook-shaped prosthetic hand is the perfect weapon of crime. While Candyman is beyond reality. But if you stand in front of the mirror and say his name five times in a row, he will cross this shaky line. And then no one will be able to escape. A group of young scientists who do not believe in the mysterious Candyman are taken up to study this legend. But at any moment, instead of being reflected in the mirror, they can see the killer ...
