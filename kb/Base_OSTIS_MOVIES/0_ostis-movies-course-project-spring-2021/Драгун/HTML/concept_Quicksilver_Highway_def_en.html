On a deserted freeway, lost in the mysterious twilight, travelers at every step await amazing and inexplicable events. Here fate has prepared a meeting with the mysterious Aaron Quicksilver for indefatigable adventurers. This fearless connoisseur of everything strange and unusual is always ready to tell guests a couple of scary stories from his inexhaustible encyclopedia of horrors and secrets. Take a look at him too, if you are not afraid that his stories will deprive you of sleep and peace for a long time
