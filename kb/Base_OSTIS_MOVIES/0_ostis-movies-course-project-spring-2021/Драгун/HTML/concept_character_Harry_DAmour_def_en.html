D'Amour is a private detective who becomes a specialist in occult affairs after repeated encounters with demonic and magical powers. he regularly faces violence, depravity and moral compromise, D'Amour tries to defend his own principles and risks his life to protect the lives and souls of others, even strangers, from suffering at the hands of demonic forces. Clive Barker, D'Amour's fate is to repeatedly resist evil forces - this is "karmic retribution" for his past sins.
