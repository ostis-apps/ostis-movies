A wealthy and extravagant student Quaid offers to help fellow student Stephen write a term paper on the nature of fear. They post announcements that they are inviting people to participate in the study. Stephen's friend Cheryl Fromm is actively helping in her work. In the process of research, students reveal their fears to each other: Stephen could be in a car in which his drunk brother crashed to death, Cheryl was raped by her father, and Quaid's family was stabbed to death by a psychopathic maniac. Stephen gets a high score for his coursework, but it's far from the end. Quaid, whom the maniac once left alive, begins to haunt the research participants, bringing their worst nightmares to life ...
