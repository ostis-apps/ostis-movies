New Orleans. Say his name five times and he will appear. An iron hook instead of a hand and bees flying around it. Once upon a time, just after the end of the American Civil War, black slave Daniel Robitaille suffered for his love for the white girl Caroline. The beloved's father, along with other wealthy landowners, cut off the hand of a slave. Death was slow and painful. This is where his nickname came from - Candyman. Nowadays, Annie's girlfriend, a direct descendant of Caroline, casts a magic spell inadvertently, and Candyman appears. Together with him, numerous corpses appear ...
