Kaedwen stretches from the Pastul Mountains in the west to the Blue Mountains in the east and from the Pontar River in the south to the city of Aedd Ginvael in the far north. Kaedwen borders Redania, Temeria, Aedirn and the states of the Hengfor League.


The formidable King Henselt often quarreled with neighboring King Demavend of Aedirn. The reason for this was the lands that are part of the Aedirn, which the fierce king considers the ancestral, ethnic possessions of Kaedwen.
