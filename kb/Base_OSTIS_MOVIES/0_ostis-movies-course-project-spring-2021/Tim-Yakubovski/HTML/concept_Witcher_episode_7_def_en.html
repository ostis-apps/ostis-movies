Geralt arrives in Cintra on the eve of the war with Nilfgaard to pick up his "Surprise Child" - Cyril, but Kalante does not give him his granddaughter and throws the witcher in prison. After the fall of the city, Geralt manages to escape. At this very time, Yennefer from Vengerberg returns to Arethusa and is present at a meeting of the council of sorcerers, whose members decide not to help Cintra against Nilfgaard. Finally, Cyrilla appears in the episode, fleeing Cintra. A group of teenagers tries to rob her, but the princess suddenly shows magical abilities.
