Radovid is one of the most powerful rulers of the North. He is a strict and stern ruler, endowed with an unbending will and a strong disposition. Radovid is very smart, a skillful politician, a brilliant strategist and tactician. The king is merciless to his enemies, but with companions and friends he is generous and complacent. He does not tolerate being lied to or being rude and disrespectful.


The king sincerely cares about the welfare of Redania: after his coronation, Radovid did everything possible to restore order in the country, which fell into decay after the death of Vizimir II. He returned the kingdom to its former power, strengthened it even more and exalted it.
