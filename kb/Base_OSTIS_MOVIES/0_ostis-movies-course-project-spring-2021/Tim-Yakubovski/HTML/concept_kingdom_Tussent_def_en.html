Toussaint is a vassal principality of Nilfgaard. This mountainous region is ruled by Princess Anna Henrietta, the Emperor's cousin.

A picturesque land southwest of Angren, near Mount Gorgon. The capital of the principality is the city of Bockler, which stands on the site of an ancient elven settlement. Thanks to the favorable location in Toussaint, the famous vineyards bloom almost all year round, and winters are warm, but, nevertheless, snowy. The climate contributes to the fact that there are always fabulous landscapes.
