Triss is a light and gentle character, she is not alien to shyness, devotion, mercy, sympathy and compassion. Triss is always eager to help people, does not refuse help, also Merigold loves people and children very much. The enchantress dislikes only those who try to take advantage of her or openly flatter, preferring to tell the truth in the face. However, conscience can make a girl look away from her interlocutor for a long time. With friends and family, Triss is sweet and good-natured, always positive and cheerful.She is possessed by a desire to achieve peace and good for everyone around, and especially for people dear to her. The girl is sometimes aware of fear, naivety and insecurity, but her dedication more than compensates for this.
