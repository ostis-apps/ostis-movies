Natural ability

He can store a tremendous amount of energy within his body, which appears to manifest in two different modes. First, despite lacking large wings, Dragonair was shown flying and without wings on the side of the head, significantly expanded. Dragonair can also discharge energy through the orbs to envelop itself in a gentle aura. It is this aura that makes his body radiant and mystical, and is said to allow Dragonair to control the weather in close proximity and change it in a matter of minutes.
