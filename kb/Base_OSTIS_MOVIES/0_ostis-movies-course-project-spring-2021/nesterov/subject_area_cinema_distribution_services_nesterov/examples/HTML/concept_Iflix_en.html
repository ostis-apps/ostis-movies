iflix is a free and subscription video on demand (VOD) service focused on emerging markets. Its global headquarters are in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. The site serves as a digital hosting and distribution platform for Western, Asian Regional and Local TV shows and movies obtained through partnerships with over 150 studios and content distributors globally. Iflix is currently available in 4 countries across Asia including Malaysia, Indonesia, the Philippines and Vietnam.[3] As of April 2020, Iflix has more than 25 million active users on its service, with over 2.5 billion minutes viewed in a month.
