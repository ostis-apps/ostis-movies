The Shawshank Redemption

Accountant Andy Dufresne is accused of murdering his wife and her lover. Once in a prison called Shawshank, he faces the cruelty and lawlessness reigning on both sides of the bars. Everyone who enters these walls becomes their slave for the rest of his life. But Andy, who has a lively mind and a good soul, finds an approach to both prisoners and guards, seeking their special favor.
