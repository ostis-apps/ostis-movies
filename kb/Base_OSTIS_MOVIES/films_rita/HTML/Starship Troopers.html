Starship Troopers

In the new Millennium, mankind is in mortal danger. Countless hordes of giant intelligent bugs from a distant system of planets in Klendathu threaten all living things in the universe. Soldier of the elite unit johnny Rico and pilot Carmen as part of the starship troopers Earthlings on the space warship Ticonderoga sent to the sinister depths of space, in the last battle to decide the fate of the Earth.
