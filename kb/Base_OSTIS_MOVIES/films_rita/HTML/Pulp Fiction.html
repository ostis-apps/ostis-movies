Pulp Fiction

Two bandits Vincent Vega and Jules Winfield lead philosophical conversations in between showdowns and solving problems with the debtors of the criminal boss Marcellus Wallace. In the first story, Vincent spends an unforgettable evening with Marcellas ' wife MIA. The second tells the story of boxer Butch Coolidge, bought by Wallace to surrender the fight. In the third story, Vincent and Jules accidentally get into trouble.
