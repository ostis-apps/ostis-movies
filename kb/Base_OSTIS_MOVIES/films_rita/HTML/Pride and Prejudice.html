Pride & Prejudice

England, late eighteenth century. The parents of the five Bennet sisters are anxious to successfully marry their daughters off. And so the regular life of a respectable family is turned upside down when a young gentleman - Mr. Bingley-appears in the neighbourhood... Of course, among the friends of the new neighbor is a lot of refined aristocrats who are not averse to court the charming sisters. However, it's not that simple. Headstrong Elizabeth meets Bingley's friend-the handsome and arrogant Mr. Darcy, and between them a serious confrontation breaks out, the result of which can be both love and hatred...
