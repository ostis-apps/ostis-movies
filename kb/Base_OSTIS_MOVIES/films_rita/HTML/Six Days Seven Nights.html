Six Days Seven Nights

A small plane crashes on a desert island. The brave pilot and his charming passenger were completely alone in the wilderness. They desperately try to report themselves, but do not know that they have long ceased to look for. Finally, for the first time in these days on the desert horizon seemed strange ship, which docked to the lost island. But instead of smiling at the unlucky couple, fate laughed at them again. Armed to the teeth, the pirates do not like the long-awaited saviors! The pilot and his companion were so eager to be in cheerful company. Now they want to be alone again...
