Home Alone 5: The Holiday Heist

After moving from Sunny California to Maine on Christmas eve, ten-year-old Finn and the youngest child in the family, feels very unusual and anxious in a new home. It seems to him that the mansion could not be more terrible. Convinced of the existence of otherworldly creatures here and for the safety of his family, he prepares traps in which evil ghosts must fall. Left alone in the house with his sister, the baby manages to activate its complicated mechanisms, which will not get ghosts...
