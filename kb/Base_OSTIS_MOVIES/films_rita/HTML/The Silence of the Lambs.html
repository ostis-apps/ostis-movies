The Silence of the Lambs

A psychopath kidnaps and kills young women across the Midwest of America. The FBI, convinced that all the crimes were committed by the same person, instructs agent Clarissa Starling to meet with a maniac inmate who could explain to the investigation the psychological motives of a serial killer and thus lead to his trail. The prisoner, psychiatrist Hannibal Lecter, is serving a sentence for murder and cannibalism. He agreed to help Clarissa only if she regaled his sick imagination with the details of her complicated personal life. Such an ambiguous relationship not only creates an internal conflict in Clarissa's soul, but also confronts her face to face with a genius-mad killer...
