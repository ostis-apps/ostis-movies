Knockin’ On Heaven’s Door

Fate brings the heroes of the picture in the hospital, where the doctors impose a death sentence on them. The time of their lives is on the clock. Further events in the film unfold at a rapid pace. After stealing a car with a million German marks in the trunk, they escape from the hospital. They are pursued by assassins, they become involuntary robbers, they are chased by the police, they get into a brothel. But nevertheless continue to rush forward, towards the destiny.
