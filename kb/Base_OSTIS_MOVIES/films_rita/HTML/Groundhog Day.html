Groundhog Day

Television commentator Phil Connors comes to a small town in Pennsylvania every year to celebrate Groundhog Day. But this time the fun risks going too far. Time had played a trick on him: it had stopped. Now Phil's calendar is blackened by the same date - February 2, from which he can not get out. The cheerful host is trying to capitalize on his comical situation: he has a lot of time ahead of him and a serene predictable future. Nothing bad will happen to him from now on... and nothing good. Phil has one cherished dream, simple and uncomplicated - February 3...
