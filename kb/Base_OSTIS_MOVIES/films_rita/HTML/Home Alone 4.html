Home Alone 4

The dream of any little bully is when parents are not at home and you have a whole house at your disposal! For Kevin McCallister, an old acquaintance of ours, those dreams are coming true. This time he is preparing to celebrate Christmas, waiting for parents and guests in a huge Superdome, stuffed with the most incredible technical devices. And believe me, the holiday will be amazing! Especially for the would-be bandits who planned to break into the house. After all, they do not yet know with whom they will have to deal...
