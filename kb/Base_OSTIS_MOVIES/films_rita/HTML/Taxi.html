Taxi

Young taxi driver Daniel is obsessed with fast driving. Like a hurricane, he sweeps through the winding streets of Marseille on his powerful roaring beast "Peugeot", frightening passengers and passers-by. Incorruptible policeman Emilien forces him to help in catching a gang of robbers, eluding the police in their elusive "Mercedes". And until the very end it is not clear who will be able to stay on a steep curve.
