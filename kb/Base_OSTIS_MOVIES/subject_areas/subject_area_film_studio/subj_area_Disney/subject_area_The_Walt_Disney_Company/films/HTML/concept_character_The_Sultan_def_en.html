Sultan - father Jasmine , ruler of Agraba. A short, good-natured old man, a simpleton with features of infantilism. Engaged in collecting miniature handmade toys, as well as various wonders. His name is unknown, but in one of the episodes his grandfather from the Afterlife World mentions his nickname “Baby Bobo”. The ancestors of the Sultan reigned in Agrabe since its foundation. In one of the series went to rescue his daughter from captivity Amazons, and the Gypsum Queen herself fell in love with him. After all the adventures, Sultan and Gypsum went on a date.
