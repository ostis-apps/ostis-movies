Iago is a reasonable talking parrot. In the past, the former assistant Jafar . In the animated film “The Return of Jafar,” he became a friend of Aladdin , but faintly betrayed him when Jafar was released from imprisonment. However, the changes were not in vain, and Iago , realizing that he does not want to be a villain, helps again Aladdin and destroys in the final Jafar , throwing his lamp into lava. By the nature of Iago , choleric, restless, often chats and annoys friends. The main flaw is greed. He attracts wealth and luxury, which often becomes the cause of his troubles, and in greed he is second only to Abismal. Often inclined to go away from the dangers, shifting the difficulties on the shoulders of friends. Prone to deception, often deceiving Aladdin , but soon repent. Despite his cowardice, it often saves Aladdin and his friends. In love with the beautiful bird Thandra, the mistress of the weather from the Rain Forest. Able to parody the voice of any person perfectly. At the end of the cartoon "Aladdin and the King of Thieves" after the wedding Aladdin leaves "Aladdin" and joins the father of Aladdin Kassim, who feels the closeness of the spirit.
