The Hatter is a hat-maker, one of the participants of the Mad Tea Party. When meeting with Alice , it behaves tactlessly, therefore, the main character asks him "not to become personal." She gives riddles to her and periodically tries to wake Mouse-Sonia. By the expression Chesyr cat , Mad Hatter is "out of your mind." Besides the fact that the character is constantly drinking tea, he sells hats and sings songs at a concert. At trial, he acted as the first witness, describing himself as a “little man,” who is as round as his hats. The character did not figure in the original version of the tale. His name and character presumably originated from the saying "Madness as a Hatter". That, in turn, was based on the fact that hats could really go crazy with the mercury vapor used to process the felt. However, Dr. med. H.
