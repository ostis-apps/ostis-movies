Cinderella

The cult cartoon Walt Disney, based on the tale of Charles Perrault. In the year of the premiere (1950), the film received a special prize from the Venice Film Festival; it remains the classic of animation in cinema. Unhappy orphan Cinderella is forced to be a servant of her domineering and vicious stepmother, as well as two stepsisters. The poor thing works from morning to evening, while her relatives roll around the balls and pick up suitors for themselves. One day, Cinderella learns that a big ball will be held in the kingdom, to which the prince himself will arrive. She really wants to get on him, but the evil stepmother does not let her.
