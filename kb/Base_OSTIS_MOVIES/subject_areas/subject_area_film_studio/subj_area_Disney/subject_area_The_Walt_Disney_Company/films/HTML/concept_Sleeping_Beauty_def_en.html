Sleeping Beauty

Long ago a king and a queen lived in a faraway country. When their long-awaited daughter Aurora was born, a big celebration was arranged in the kingdom so that everyone could greet the little one. Three good fairies were invited to the ball - Flora, Fauna and Good Weather, but they forgot about the evil witch. Offended, she cursed the princess - on the day of her 16th birthday, Aurora would inject herself about the spindle and fall asleep with an eternal sleep. The witchcraft was so strong that the good fairies could not destroy it. But still they saved the princess: she would wake up from sleep after the kiss of the beautiful prince ...
