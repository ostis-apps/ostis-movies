Mulan

Difficult times came for a great nation: a warlike Hun tribe attacked the country. Having changed into men's clothes, Mulan joins other soldiers and sets off on a dangerous expedition to the foot of the snow-capped mountains. She is accompanied by a funny dragon Musha, who looks more like a little dog than a mythical monster. Trying to hide a secret, they get into funny situations and do not suspect that their secret is about to be revealed! ...
