Caterpillar - a blue and three-inch insect. He sits on a white mushroom and smokes a hookah. Tip Caterpillars about that one should always keep oneself in the hands of an expressive parody of the main reception of moralizing literature for children of the early nineteenth century. According to the philosopher Peter Heath, Caterpillar adheres to the philosophical views of Locke on the immutability of personality, which is expressed in the stability of memory. The person is aware of himself as such, because he remembers his own past.
