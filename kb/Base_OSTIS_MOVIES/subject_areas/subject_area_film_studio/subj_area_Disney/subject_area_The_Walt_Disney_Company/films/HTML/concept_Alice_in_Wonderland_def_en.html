Alice in Wonderland

Having run away from home, Alice saw a large white rabbit wearing an old jacket and vest under an old apple tree. Pulling a watch out of his pocket, the rabbit hastily disappeared into a suspiciously dark hole. Having gathered courage, Alice took a step after him ... and got into a wonderful fairy-tale world, where the most unrealistic fantasies come true. To get back home, Alice will have to make a journey through this whole strange and bizarre world, in which absolutely incredible adventures and absolutely fantastic creatures await her ...
