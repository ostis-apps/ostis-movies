Cristin Milioti

American actress and singer. She has worked in Broadway theatre productions such as That Face, Stunning, and the Tony Award-winning musical Once. She also played the titular Mother on the sitcom How I Met Your Mother from 2013 to 2014, Teresa Petrillo Belfort in the 2013 film The Wolf of Wall Street, and Betsy Solverson in the second season of Fargo.
