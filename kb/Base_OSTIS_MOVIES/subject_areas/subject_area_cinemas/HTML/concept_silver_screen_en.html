Silver Screen cinema - a network of cinemas with high-tech equipment, comfortable seating and tasty movie snacks. In Silver Screen you can watch:
  • Films in the original with subtitles;
  • Films in Belarusian;
  • Sports broadcasts;
  • Music concerts in the halls with the Dolby Atmos system;
  • Pre-shows before the release of the film in wide distribution;
  • Screenings with movie stars;
  • Shows of retrospectives and arthouse;
  • Broadcasts of operas and theatrical productions of the best theaters in the world;
  • Documentary film about the masterpieces of world art.
