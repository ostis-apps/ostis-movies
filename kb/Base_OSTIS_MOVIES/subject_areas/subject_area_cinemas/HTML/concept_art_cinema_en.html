ArtCinema opened for its viewers July 15, 2015. It is located on the 3rd floor of the shopping and entertainment center "Titan" at the metro station Petrovschina. In the cinema there are 2 movie theaters equipped with the latest technology, which can comfortably accommodate 320 viewers. The ArtCinema has all the technical innovations in the field of film screening, be it digital equipment or equipment for showing films in 3D.
