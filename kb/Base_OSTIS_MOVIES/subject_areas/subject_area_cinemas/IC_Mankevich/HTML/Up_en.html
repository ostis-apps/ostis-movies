78-year-old grumbler Karl Fredriksen believes that life bypasses him. To keep his promise to his deceased wife, he decides to fulfill his dream of a great adventure by tying thousands of balloons to his home and flying into the wilds of South America. Without flying and half a mile, the traveler discovers that he inadvertently brought along an extremely talkative and incorrigibly cheerful 8-year-old boy named Russell...
