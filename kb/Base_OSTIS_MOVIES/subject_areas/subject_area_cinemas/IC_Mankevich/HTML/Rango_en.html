Rango

Rango is a chameleon who lives in a terrarium and considers himself a hero, who, unfortunately, does not manage to show his fearlessness. But when he suddenly finds himself in the town of Dirt, he has such an opportunity. Rango proclaims himself a fighter for justice and begins to act like a sheriff in the Wild West. He still does not know that being a “good guy” in these parts is not the most enviable fate...
