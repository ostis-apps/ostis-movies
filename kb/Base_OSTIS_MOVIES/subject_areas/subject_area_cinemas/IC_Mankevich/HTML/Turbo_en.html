Turbo

Nothing irritates the temperamental, dreaming of speed snail with the speaking name Turbo, like a leisurely life, slow conversations, slowed down neighbors. Let our hero look like a big eccentric in this sluggish, leisurely world, let him go against the law of nature about the “born to crawl”, but if the “fiery motor” burns in the heart, and the soul believes in a miracle, it simply has no right not to happen!
