Ice Age

The action takes place 20 thousand years ago. To avoid approaching due to the onset of the ice age of the cold, animals migrate south. However, some of them still decide to stay - the lonely, gloomy mammoth Manfred, as well as reckless sloth Sid. By chance this couple comes across a human cub. They decide to return it to people and go on a trip. Along the way, they meet a saber-toothed cunning tiger. And now this fun company will have fun adventures!
