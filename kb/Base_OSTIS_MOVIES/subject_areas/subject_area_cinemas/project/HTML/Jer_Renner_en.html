Jeremy Lee Renner- American actor , two-time nominee for the award " Oscar ”(2010, 2011), nominee for the BAFTA (2010) and Golden Globes (2011). The most famous films with Renner's participation: “The Lord of the Storm” (2008), “City of Thieves” (2010), “Mission Impossible: Phantom Protocol” (2011), “Avengers” (2012), “The Bourne Evolution” (2012), “ Witch Hunters ”(2013),“ Avengers: Age of Ultron ”(2015),“ First Avenger: Confrontation ”(2016),“ Windy River ”(2017),“ Avengers: Final ”(2019).
