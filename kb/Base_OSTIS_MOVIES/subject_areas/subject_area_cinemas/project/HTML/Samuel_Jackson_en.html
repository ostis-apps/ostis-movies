Samuel Leroy Jackson - American actor film and television, producer . In 1991, he received critical acclaim for his role in Tropical Fever, and then appeared in films such as Good Fellows, Patriot Games, and Jurassic Park. In 1994, he starred in the movie Pulp Fiction, which brought him to the level of superstars and brought several awards. Since then, Jackson has starred in more than 100 films and has won many awards throughout his career.
