Peter Pan

Walt Disney invites us to the magical land of Neverland, where lives an amazing boy who can fly and refuses to grow up. This is a funny inventor, brave and mischievous Peter pan! One day, in search of his own shadow, Peter finds himself in the London of our world and becomes a friend of little Wendy and her brothers Michael and John, and, of course, he takes his night friends with him to Neverland, to meet amazing adventures. The heroes will meet with the fairy tinkerbell and the friendly gang of Peter pan, save the Indian Princess Tiger Lily from the clutches of pirates and fight with their demonic leader captain Hook!..
