The Incredibles 2

After the events of the first part of the superfamily enjoys increased attention from journalists. Suddenly, the charming Mrs. Exceptional looked much better on the TV screen than her husband. Mr. Exceptional increasingly has to stay at home and look after children, each of whom has their own reasons to be dissatisfied with their own superpowers. However, family problems will take a back seat when it turns out that the peace of the Superfamily is threatened by a powerful new enemy.
