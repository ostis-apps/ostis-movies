Finding Nemo

Among the beautiful tropical sea elements, in the area of the Great barrier reef, a clown fish named Marlin lives in seclusion. He is raising his only son, Nemo. The ocean and the dangers that exist in it are very scary to Marlin, and he protects his son from them as much as possible, but young Nemo, suffering from excessive curiosity, very much wants to find out more about the mysterious reef near which they live. When Nemo ironically finds himself far from home, and still faces the threat of becoming the dinner of a tank fish, Marlin goes in search of his son...
