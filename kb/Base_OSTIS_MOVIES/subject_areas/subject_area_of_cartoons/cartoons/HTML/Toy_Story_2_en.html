Toy Story 2

Cowboy woody and other toys of a boy named Andy continue to live, enjoying every day. But suddenly everything changes when woody is kidnapped by a treacherous collector. It turns out that woody is a very valuable toy from the collection, for which the Japanese toy Museum is willing to pay a huge amount. In the collector's house, he finds his relatives. At this time, toys led by buzz Lightyear are sent to rescue woody. Woody learns about his famous past, buzz and the other toys go through a dangerous and thorny path. Woody faces a difficult question - what is its purpose? To Shine in the Museum window or to return to your favorite owner?
