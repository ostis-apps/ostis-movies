Teri Lynn Hatcher

American actress, voice-actress, writer, singer, YouTuber, and former National Football League cheerleader. Also as Lois Lane on the television series Lois & Clark: The New Adventures of Superman, as Paris Carver in the James Bond film Tomorrow Never Dies, and as Susan Mayer on the television series Desperate Housewives, for which she won the Golden Globe Award for Best Actress in a Musical or Comedy, three Screen Actors Guild Awards, and a Primetime Emmy nomination for Outstanding Lead Actress in a Comedy Series.
