Full_House_Take_2

This series pivots around a hapkido teacher Jang Man Ok becoming a stylist of top idol group TAKE ONE, composed of the perfectionist Lee Tae Ik and a free yet kind guy named Won Kang Hwi and getting into a romantic relationship with them. Tae Ik became an idol in order to gain back the house "Full House" where he had lived with his late parents. Can Tae Ik regain "Full House"? And what about these three's future in love...?
