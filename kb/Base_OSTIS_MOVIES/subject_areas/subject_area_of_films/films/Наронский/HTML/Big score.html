The four-fingered Frankie was to smuggle the stolen diamond from England to the USA to his boss Evie. But instead, the hero gets into the epicenter of big trouble. Making a bet on an underground boxing match, Frankie falls into the cycle of very undesirable events. A complex intrigue is unfolded around the hero and his cargo with the participation of many colorful characters of the London bottom - the Russian gangster, three unlucky robbers, a cunning boxer and a sullen burglar of a formidable mafia. Each strives alone to disrupt the Big Kush.
