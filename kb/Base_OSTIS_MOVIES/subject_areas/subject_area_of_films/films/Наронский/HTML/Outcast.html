The hero of this film, Chuck Noland, is an employee of the world famous Federal Express delivery service. Chuck is a scrupulous practitioner and an incorrigible pedant. The life of Noland, a high-ranking inspector of international branches of FedEx, is painted by the minute. And these precious minutes are sorely lacking either for privacy or for the woman she loves ... However, the merciless fate forced Noland to take a different look at the time allotted to him ... The plane on which Chuck is traveling falls into the ocean. The engineer, the only one who was lucky enough to stay alive, falls on an uninhabited island. Here Noland will have to spend painfully long years in a fierce battle for survival ...
