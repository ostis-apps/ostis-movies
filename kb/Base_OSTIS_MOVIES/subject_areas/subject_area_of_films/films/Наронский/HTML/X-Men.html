They are the children of the atomic age, supermen, a new link in the chain of evolution. Each of them was born as a result of a unique genetic mutation that has endowed them with extraordinary abilities since childhood. Under the guidance of Professor Charles Xavier, a world-renowned telepath, gifted students learned to control and manage their amazing abilities in the interests of humanity. But not all mutants share the views of the professor: the powerful mutant Magneto, who controls all metals, assembled a team of like-minded people. He does not believe that people and mutants can ever coexist peacefully. They developed a plan to seize power over the world, and then, only students of Professor Xavier will be able to protect this world ...
