In the Great Roman Empire there was no military leader equal to the common Maximus. The invincible legions commanded by this noble warrior will worship him and will follow him even to hell. But it so happened that the brave Maximus, who was ready to fight any opponent in a fair fight, was powerless against the perfidious court intrigues. Common betrayed and sentenced to death. Miraculously escaped death, Maximus becomes a gladiator. Quickly gained fame in bloody battles, he finds himself in the famous Roman Coliseum, in the arena of which he will meet in a mortal battle with his sworn enemy ...
