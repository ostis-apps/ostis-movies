2012 - disaster film directed by Roland Emmerich, released in 2009. The film's title is based on the date December 21, 2012, which played a role in the Mayan calendar. The film premiered in the USA on November 12, 2009, and in Russia on November 13, 2009. Distributor — Columbia Pictures. Production — Centropolis Entertainment (USA) and The Bridge Studios (Canada)
