Krid 2 - american sports drama film with Michael B. Jordan and Sylvester Stallone in the lead roles, which is the eighth in the series "rocky". The plot continues the story of the films "creed: the Legacy of rocky" and "rocky 4", returning to the screens of characters such as Ivan Drago, Ludmila Drago and Robert Balboa performed by Dolph Lundgren, Brigitte Nielsen and Milo ventimigli respectively.
