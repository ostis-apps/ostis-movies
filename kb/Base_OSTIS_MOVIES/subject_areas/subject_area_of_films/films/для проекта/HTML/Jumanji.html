Jumanji

Four teenagers find themselves inside the Jumanji game. They will fight with a rhinoceros, black Mambo, and every step will be watched by an endless series of traps and puzzles. To complete the game and stay alive, they will have to reincarnate in the characters of the game: timid and shy Spencer turns into a brave and strong researcher, big Fridge — in Shorty, fashionista and beauty bettani — a Professor, and clumsy and awkward Martha becomes fearless and agile Amazon. Friends will have to get used to a completely new and so unusual for themselves roles and find their way home.
