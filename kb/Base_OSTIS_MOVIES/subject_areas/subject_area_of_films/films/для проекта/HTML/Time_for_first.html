Time for first

60's. the Height of the cold war. Two superpowers, the USSR and the United States are fighting for supremacy in the space race. While the USSR is ahead, the next step is a man's spacewalk. Two weeks before the launch, a test ship explodes. There is no time to identify the causes. And even though the risks are enormous, we cannot give up leadership. Experienced military pilot Pavel Belyaev and his partner Alexei Leonov, unarmed and hot, dreaming of a feat-two people ready to step into the unknown. But no one could even imagine all that they had to face in flight. In this mission, all that is possible, went wrong…
