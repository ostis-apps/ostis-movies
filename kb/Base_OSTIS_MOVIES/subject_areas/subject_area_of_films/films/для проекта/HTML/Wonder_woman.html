Wonder woman

Before becoming a Wonder Woman, she was Diana, a Princess of the Amazons, trained to be an invincible warrior. And when on the shores of a Paradise island fenced from the outside world, which served as her homeland, an American pilot crashes and talks about a serious conflict raging in the outside world, Diana leaves her home to cope with this threat. And there, fighting side by side with man in the war for peace, Diana will discover the fullness of her power... and her true destiny.
