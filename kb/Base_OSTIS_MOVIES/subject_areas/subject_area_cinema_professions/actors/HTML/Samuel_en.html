Samuel El Jackson - american film and television actor, producer. In 1991, he received critical acclaim for his role in the film "Tropical fever", and then appeared in films such as "Nice guys", "Games of patriots" and "Jurassic Park". In 1994 he starred in the film "Pulp fiction", which brought him to the level of superstars and won several awards. Since then, Jackson has starred in more than 100 films and won numerous awards throughout his career.
