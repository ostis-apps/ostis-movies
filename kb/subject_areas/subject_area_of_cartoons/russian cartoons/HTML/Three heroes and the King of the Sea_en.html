Three heroes and the King of the Sea

Bogatyrs have family troubles. To unwind, they decide to quickly drive to China for the dragon's tooth, a symbol of wisdom and strength. At the same time, the prince of Kiev goes for a walk with the horse Julius for the treasures of the king of the sea to replenish the treasury and "patch the budget." Why, only the king of the sea decided to marry and for this drowned Kiev. And how to be now? What will help our heroes? Friendship, courage and, of course, love.
