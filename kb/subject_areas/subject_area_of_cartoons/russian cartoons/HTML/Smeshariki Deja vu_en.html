Smeshariki. Deja vu

Krosh decides to arrange an unforgettable birthday Kopatycha - he turns to the agency "Deja vu", which promises its customers amazing time travel. But because of non-compliance with the terms of the contract, all smeshariki scatters in time, and Krosh will have to gather friends from different eras in the company of his second “I” - the matured and grumbling Shorq, who appeared due to a temporary paradox.
