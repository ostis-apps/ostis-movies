Alyosha Popovich and Tugarin Snake

When the enemy horde approached Rostov, headed by Tugarin the Serpent, Alyosha Popovich failed to save the inhabitants from paying tribute. Now the hero must restore the good name, return the gold and drive the enemy out of the Russian land. He goes on a journey, and Uncle Tikhon, the girl Lyubava, her grandmother and the talking horse Julius will help him in this.
