The Snow Queen: The Looking Glass

The mighty King Harald almost lost his family because of the Snow Queen. He finds a way to clear the world of magic - all owners of magical abilities are now sharpened through the looking glass. Stop it and not let the fairy tale disappear forever from our world can only Gerda. After all, her main strength is not in magic, but faith in good and friendship. In the new adventure, trolls, pirates and even the Snow Queen herself will come to the aid of Gerda.
