Alice's Birthday

The film "Alice's Birthday" tells about the extraordinary adventures of the girl Alice and her friend - the little archeologist Rrrr in the past of the distant alien planet Koleida. Alice and Rrrr show courage, ingenuity and endurance, learn not to lose heart in the most hopeless situations, and learn what real friendship is. As a result, they managed to change the course of history and bring the whole planet back to life!
