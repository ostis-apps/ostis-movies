Story About Petr and Fevrony_en

XIII century. The villain seized power in the principality of Murom. Only the brave warrior Peter goes to battle with the villain and defeats him. But the poisonous blood of the sorcerer poison the savior of the principality. The healer Fevronia is ready to use her gift to save Peter. Between young people, there is a real pure feeling that they have to preserve, despite many obstacles.
