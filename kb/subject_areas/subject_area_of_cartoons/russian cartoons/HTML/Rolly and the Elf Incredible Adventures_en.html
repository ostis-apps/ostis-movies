Rolly and the Elf: Incredible Adventures

Somewhere far away, in the dense Scandinavian forests there is a lively fairytale folk - Rolly. Rolly has no worries, and her favorite activity is to arrange each other funny, though not always harmless, practical jokes. But the carefree life suddenly ends! In the forest comes a terrible misfortune, threatening to destroy all its inhabitants. The only way to salvation is to find the magic heart, but the path to it is full of dangerous adventures - only friendship and devotion will help to pass it and save the magic country from destruction!
