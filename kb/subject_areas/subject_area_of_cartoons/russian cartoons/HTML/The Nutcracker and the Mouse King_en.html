The Nutcracker and the Mouse King

The story of the Nutcracker Prince and Masha is a story of loyalty and courage, magic and luck, fairytale transformations and human feelings. The capricious callous Prince is turned into an ugly doll, and only the pure loving heart of a little girl is able to free him from evil spells. The prince and Masha become true friends and together overcome the evil intrigues of Myshilda and her nephew - the Mouse King.
