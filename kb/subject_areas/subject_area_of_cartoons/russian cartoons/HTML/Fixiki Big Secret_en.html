Fixiki: Big Secret

Fiksiki are little kind people who live in machines and devices and take care of technology. But people should not know about the fix! This is a big secret! Fixies find a way to move through electrical wires. Now they can instantly fix any device, wherever it is! And here the unfinished invention falls to the mischievous Fire. In the hands of a small fix it causes big problems. Sparks, explosions, destruction, enraged technology - the city on the verge of disaster! Fixikam almost miraculously able to prevent it. But how to keep them a BIG SECRET ?!
