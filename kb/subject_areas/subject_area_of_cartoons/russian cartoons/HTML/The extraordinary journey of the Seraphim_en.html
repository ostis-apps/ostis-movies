The extraordinary journey of the Seraphim

Sima Resurrection, who lost her parents at the very beginning of the war, lives in an orphanage and secretly keeps a neck cross, reminding her of her beloved family. One day Sima meets Rita, a girl who reveals to her that the house is full of secrets and ghosts, and invites her to go to the mysterious closet under the stairs to look at one of them. Who would have thought that the invitation would help the Seraphim to reveal the secret of the orphanage and shed light on the fate of the parents ...
