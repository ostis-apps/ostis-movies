Puss in Boots

The cat in a bag arrives in Russia as part of humanitarian aid from America and is taken away by a young drunkard and would-be patriot Karabasov, who is offered to emigrate to America. The flight on the magic cat, which uses the tail as a propeller, ends over Europe. There, an arrogant young man forgets about the invaluable services that Puss in Boots has provided him, and he returns the ungrateful back to Russia ...
