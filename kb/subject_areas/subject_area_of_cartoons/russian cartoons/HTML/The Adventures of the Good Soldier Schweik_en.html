The Adventures of the Good Soldier Schweik

Josef Svejk, a Czech dog dealer who was already disqualified from the army as a uniform idiot, is again called to serve as cannon fodder during the First World War. He was bothered by bureaucrats, policemen, doctors and clergymen, he was “sold” from one officer to another, but the good-natured, talkative and ingenuous soldier Svejk still reluctantly approaches the front line and, obviously, to certain death.
