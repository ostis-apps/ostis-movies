Phaser - the signal is split, part of it is filtered by a phase filter to create a phase shift, after which the filtered and unfiltered signals are mixed. The phaser effect is similar to the flanger effect, but is difficult to reproduce on analog equipment. A phaser is used to achieve "synthesis" or "electronicization" of natural sounds such as human speech. The voice of the character C-3PO from the Star Wars film was created by phaser editing the actor's voice.
