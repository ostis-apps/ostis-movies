Studio microphones are acoustics that are used in the studio. This is not only about professional sound recording, but also about radio stations, television studios, broadcasting points. In one studio, several types of microphones can be installed, which differ in technical characteristics, sizes and purposes, but they will all be studio ones. Microphones are used to convert an acoustic wave into an electronic signal. At the heart of this equipment is a thin membrane that perceives sound vibrations in the air. The movement of the membrane excites electrical vibrations, which create a signal.
