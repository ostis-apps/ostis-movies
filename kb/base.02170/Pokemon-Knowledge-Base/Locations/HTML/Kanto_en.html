Kanto

Kanto is the first region introduced in the pokemon universe. He is followed by Johto, Hoenn, Sinnoh, Unova, Kalos, Alola, and Galar. To the east of Kanto lies Johto, forming a small continent; to the south are the Orange Islands and to the southwest is Hoenn. Sinnoh is believed to be north of Kanto. Where Kalos, Unova and Galar are from the Kanto side is unknown.
