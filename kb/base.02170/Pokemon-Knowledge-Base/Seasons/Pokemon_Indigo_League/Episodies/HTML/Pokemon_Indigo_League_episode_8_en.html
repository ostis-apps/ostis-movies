Ep.8: Road to Pokemon League

Ash goes to Vermilion to defeat the stadium leader there. On the way, he meets various coaches and he defeats them, scoring as many as ten victories in a row. One of the coaches admires Ash's strength and tells him about AJ - a strong coach who never lost and even founded his own stadium. Ash goes there and discovers that A.J. has already defeated other coaches 98 times in a row. He challenges him to a fight, which he loses. Ash does not want to accept defeat and decides to find out the secret of AJ's strength and looks into his tent, where he sees the gym and his hard training Pokémon. Ash begins to reproach AJ with cruel treatment of Pokémon and even tries to incite the latter to riot. To which AJ retorts that he is demanding of Pokémon as well as of himself and, once setting the task of becoming the best, he will achieve his goal by all means. While Ash is arguing with A.J., Team R steals A.J.'s Sandshrew (thinking it is Ash's Pikachu). They try to escape with him, but Sandshrew breaks free and comes back. Team R comes after him and ends up challenging AJ, who loses. So A.J. wins the 100th victory and goes to get the badges, hoping to meet Ash again.
