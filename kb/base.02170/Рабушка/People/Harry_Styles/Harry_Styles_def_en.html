Harry Edward Styles is an English singer, songwriter and actor. In early March 2016, it became known that Harry Styles will play one of the main roles in the film "Dunkirk" directed by Christopher Nolan. Harry Styles appeared as Eros, Thanos' brother, in a scene after the credits of Chloe Zhao's "The Eternals". This will be the first appearance of Stiles and his character in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Harry played the main role in Olivia Wilde's upcoming psychological thriller "Don't Worry, Honey". Currently, Harry Styles, along with Emma Corrin, has completed filming in the Amazon Studios romantic drama "My Policeman", based on the 2012 novel of the same name. The director of the film will be Michael Grandage.
