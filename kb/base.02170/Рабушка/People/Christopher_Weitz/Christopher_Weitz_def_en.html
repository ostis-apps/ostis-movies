Christopher John Weitz is an American film director, screenwriter, producer and actor. He is best known for his work with his brother, Paul Weitz, with whom he worked on the comedy films "American Pie" (1999) and "American Pie 2" (2001), as well as as a director and screenwriter of the film adaptation of the novel "Golden Compass". In 2009, Chris worked on the film "Twilight. The saga. New Moon", a continuation of the sensational vampire saga "Twilight" (author Stephanie Mayer).
