John David Washington is an American football player, film and television actor. He first appeared on the screen at the age of eight in the movie "Malcolm X", where his father played the main role. From 2015 to 2019, he played one of the main roles in the TV series "Players". In 2018, he played in the movie "Black Klansman". For this role, he was nominated for a Golden Globe and a Screen Actors Guild Award. In this film, Washington was noticed by Christopher Nolan and invited to the main role in the film "Tenet". .
