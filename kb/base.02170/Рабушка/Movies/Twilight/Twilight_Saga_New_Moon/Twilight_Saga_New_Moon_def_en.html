Falling in love with a vampire is scary and romantic. But to lose a loved one who decided to save his girlfriend from the role of a pawn in the eternal confrontation of the "night hunters" clans at the cost of a break is simply unbearable. Bella Swan is painfully experiencing the disappearance of Edward and unsuccessfully seeks oblivion in friendship with the Indian boy Jacob Black. She does not even suspect that her best friend is a product of another "people of Darkness". A people far more cruel and dangerous than the vampire aristocrats...
