Seventeen-year-old girl Bella moves to her father in the small town of Forks. She falls in love with a mysterious classmate who, as it turns out, comes from a family of vampires who refused to attack people. Fall in love with a vampire. Is it scary? It's romantic, it's beautiful and painful, but it can't end well, especially in the eternal confrontation of vampire clans, where the slightest difference from others already turns you into an enemy.
