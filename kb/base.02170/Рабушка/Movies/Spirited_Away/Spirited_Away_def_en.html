Chihiro and Mom and Dad are moving into a new house. After getting lost on the way, they find themselves in a strange deserted city, where a magnificent feast awaits them. Parents greedily pounce on food and to the horror of the girls turn into pigs, becoming prisoners of the evil sorceress Yubaba. Now, finding herself alone among magical creatures and mysterious visions, Chihiro must figure out how to rid her parents of the charms of the insidious old woman.
